About me

Hi, my name is Abdelaziz Mokhnache. I am a Javascript developer.

Over the last two years, I was involved in the web development industry loving Javascript programming language and all it relates to.

By following senior developers’ advices and struggling with confusing parts of the language, I came to collect a strong understanding of the core language including the most misunderstood topics that a lot of developers just avoid, like coersion, closures and prototypes.

Once I felt confortable wih the language, I moved toward learning code optimization and design patterns following the guidelines of well-known names in the JS community like “Douglas Crockford”, “Nicholas C Zakas” and so on.

When I’m not writing or debugging JS code, you will find me reading arcticles and books about code optimization and best practices that professional developers do.
